My Portfolio

A showcase of my abilities, experience and interests

By Margaret Snape

Short Summary

I have always had a keen interest in computing, studying it at university and going into financial software development. I am an skilled developer with over 14 years of experience in large corporations.

Experience

I have over 14 years of experience, primarily in analytical quant seftware development in investment banks. My roles included taking responsibility for many aspects of different projects including design, development, testing and providing demos and support to clients.

Predominantly working in Fixed Income in pricing and risk, I was also able to look at Swaps and Foreign Exchange instruments.

Technical Skills

I have a Computer Science with Business Studies degree from Birmingham University and while working completed a part-time degree in Mathematics with the Open University.

In my work I have used C++ extensively as well as Java. For my last role I needed learn Scala and enjoyed working in this new language. I have worked in OO design as well as functional programming.

I have used many different types of version control, in particular Subversion and Git. I have worked in environments where we made heavy use of Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery.

I love to keep up-to-date and am currently taking Coursera courses in Python and Statistics.

Transferable Skills

I enjoy learning and will always be looking into new areas and new skills. I am a social person and enjoy getting to know classmates and colleagues. I love to problem-solve and have a passion for Mathematics. I am enthusiastic and creative and bring this to my work and other interests.

I have good organisational skills in work as well as at home as I enjoy being busy and through good organisation find I am able to try lots of new activities.

I have learnt much through volunteering, such as empathy, different perspectives and have always enjoyed the new opportunities.

Motivation to return to technology

Now my children are in childcare and school I am looking forward to getting back to development and using my skills to problem solve. I have have continued to learn within my break and am excited to put these skills into use as well as continue to develop them.

I am keen to use the many skills I have learned so far and apply them to other industries where I can make an impact on both the company and people.

I am keen to also use my experience and skills to help others in their careers.
